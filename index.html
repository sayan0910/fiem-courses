Admission Process

For B.Tech

  • Admission for B.Tech courses will be done through West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination (WBJEE). This means that candidates have to appear in WBJEE and qualify it.
  • Qualified candidates have to register for the counselling process by paying the counselling fee.
  • The seats will be offered according to the merit of the WBJEE marks of the candidates and choices made by them in the counselling process.
  • Selected candidates have to report to college along with the token amount (recommended by the Central Selection Committee).

For BBA

  • Candidates have to apply for the BBA programme through an online application process.
  • Admission is done on merit basis i.e marks obtained by the candidates in 10+2 examination.
  • List of the selected will be released by the college on its official website.
  • Selected candidates have to report to the college along with their relevant documents, get their documents verified and pay the fee.

For MBA

  • Candidates have to apply for the MBA programme on the college website.
  • Candidates also have to appear in any national-level management entrance examination which is MAT/CMAT/JEMAT.
  • Candidates who have applied for the MBA programme along with a valid score in the entrance examination have to appear in the group discussion and personal interview rounds.
  • Final selection will be made on the basis of examination scores, academic credentials, work experience, performance in group discussion and personal interview.

For BCA

  • Application form for BCA programme will be available in online mode.
  • Admission to the BCA programme is done on the basis of marks obtained by the candidates in the 10+2 examination. Candidates should have Mathematics/Statistics/Computer Science as one of the subjects in their 10+2 examination.

For MCA

  • Candidates have to first apply online on the college official website.
  • Candidates seeking admission to MCA courses must have qualified Joint Entrance Examination for Admission to Master of Computer Applications Course (JECA). Candidates also should have Mathematics as a subject of their graduation.
  • Final selection will be made on the basis of marks obtained by the candidates in JECA.
